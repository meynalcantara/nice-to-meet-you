# nice-to-meet-you
Portfolio of data analytics projects and learning journey towards becoming a Junior Data Analyst

Hi, my name is **Charmaine**, and I am learning to become a ***Junior Data Analyst***.

I worked for almost 10 years as a chef in hotels, restaurants, and airline catering in Berlin and Munich.

While working as a chef, I noticed I was often working with numbers and data. For example, I tracked stock, checked costs, and helped reduce waste in the kitchen. This made me interested in data and how it can improve work.

My key skills are working with data like inventory and costs, and I am now learning tools like Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

I also have strong transferable skills from my kitchen experience, such as working under pressure, being very organized, paying attention to detail, and solving problems quickly.

Now I want to start my career as a Junior Data Analyst and use my experience and new skills in a data role.
